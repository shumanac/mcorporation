As our core value at M Corporation USA Ltd we have a rich history of corporate social responsibility; our non-profit unit has grown and evolved over the past years to meet the demand of the present complexities of today's problems and global challenges.

Our non-profit's wing primary concern and goal is delivering food to the less fortunate in our community, our way to achieve this goal has various steps. To bring the food to the people who need it most, we accumulate food, by going by various hotels and restaurants who participate in our program and pick up all the food the can spare, after having picking up the food we drive to our set destination to hand out food to the less fortunate in and around our community.

We take corporate social responsibilities very serious and are committed to making a change where possible, currently 15% of M Corporation's profits are directly put into the Feed the Poor and Hungry project.

Feed the Poor and Hungry People is a non-profit charitable project that was set up by M Corporation to feed and making sure the less privileged around the world get access to nutritious food.

As its core value we at M Corporation and all its employees don't believe in wasting food and try to raise awareness of the inaccessibility of so many unfortunate to the basic necessity of life, food.

M Corporation strives to rid the world of hunger and poverty by taking the lead in this project and trying to convince other companies to join our cause, not just for ourselves but for the betterment of our planet.
