Mission Statements







  has been a renowned US based Construction Company for decades and is one of US's leading and most experienced construction companies. Killian has gained a lot of experience, expertise and respect in the US and globally and has many projects under the belt ranging from retail sites to golf courses.

Killian Group has a wide range of projects in other industries such as commercial, entertainment, hospitality, education, healthcare, industrial and various other industries. The Killian group has experience in all forms of construction delivery systems and is sure to cater to the needs of every client in every way they can. It is also specializes in cost plus fixed fee (CPFF) and design/build (D/B) forms of contracting.

M Corporation USA Ltd. merged with Killian Construction as a strategic partner for the Middle East and Asia-Pacific region. Our jointly mission is to provide clients with quality construction services, which have resulted in an over 80% repeat clientele business. M Corporation USA Ltd. and Killian Construction's merger has lead to an international commercial construction services firm, which is highly respected as a leader in "team approach" project delivery.

We are able to offer our clients advice on single rooms, whole floors or full project management, giving our clients the much needed time, space and freedom to run their hotel. M Corporation is dedicated to offering the highest standard and best service to our clients in any field of construction we can.

Together, M Corporation USA Ltd and Killian Group are the ideal choice for construction services in the Asia and Middle East regions.
