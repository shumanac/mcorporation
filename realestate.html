Mission Statement









  is a US based real estate development company, which has been operating on a national level for over 20 years as one of the Midwestern regional leading real estate developers.

Vogel Properties has built up a strong reputation creating a strong and faithful clientele base, with many repeat clients and an ever growing list of new clients.

With a strong product name in the real estate industry Vogel Properties is known to deliver to residential and corporate clientele, it is capable of taking on any size project.

Our Real Estate Division having merged with Vogel Properties as a strategic partner provide full-service residential & commercial real estate development and expertise for its customers in the Midwestern region of the USA and the Middle East and Asia-Pacific region. As a privately-held entity, we are free of boundaries when it comes to creative design, construction, leasing development and financing solutions.

As embedded in our company's philosophy we are highly dedicated to delivering unparallel customer service, M Corporation USA Ltd. proactively breaks down barriers and secures entitlements to simplify each client's real estate needs, and take away our clients worries.

Our jointly mission and goal is to exceed our clients' expectations and by doing so deliver the most unprecedented real estate development solutions for each unique situation and each unique client.

We are more than committed to our goal of providing the utmost service and all the necessary information to ensure that our clients are reassured they have a reliable local partner they can trust.
