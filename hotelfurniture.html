Mission Statement








  Furniture was established in the mid 80's making it one of Bangladesh's oldest and most experienced wooden furniture manufacturers.

Founded by Mr. Rahman, Hatil Furniture has developed into a major player not only in Bangladesh but internationally as well. Hatil specializes mostly in wooden furniture and has become an exclusive supplier to many international brand name hotels in Bangladesh and abroad.

With a massive yearly turnover Hatil is sure to reach even what seemed impossible before heights, and will be sure to become a household name in many other international brand name hotels in the following years.

M Corporation USA Ltd. having merged with Hatil as a strategic partner has become a professional office furniture group enterprise which integrates the design, development, manufacturing and marketing of high grade hotel furniture. M Corporation USA Ltd. and Hatil strive to develop and manufacture modern design furniture, closely following the latest trends in international hotel furniture and expanding the design concept beyond the traditional.

M Corporation USA Ltd. and Hatil are renowned for the design, development, manufacturing and sales of top quality and durable case goods and upholstered furniture.

Over the years we have developed and upheld successful working relationships with purchasing companies, designing firms and hotel companies. Our clientele includes top brand hotels such as Starwood, Carlson, Marriot, IHG and Hilton group, amongst countless other well-known brand hotels.

The merger between Hatil and M Corporation USA Ltd. combines many years of experience on both sides of the hotel industry into a strong corporate and profitable venture.
