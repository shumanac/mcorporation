About M Corporation

Originally based in Orange County, California USA M Corporation USA Ltd. was established in 2000 by founding president Terry Hashemee, and with now various overseas office located in Bangladesh and China.

Established with a hospitality focus, M Corporation USA Ltd. has grown into many other sectors, applying the same knowledge and expertise of the hospitality industry into the other sectors it currently specializes in, with numerous other international companies.

M Corporation USA Ltd. offers the following services:

  • Hotel Division
  • Real-Estate
  • Energy and Power
  • Construction
  • Hotel Furniture
  • Apparel
  • Non-profit Organization

With years of acquired knowledge and a specialized team of experts from around the world, M Corporation is driven by its commitment to taking customer satisfaction to a whole new level by transforming the client's visions to reality. Our company offers many opportunities and modern solutions to companies in various sectors such as interior design, construction, furniture designing and manufacturing, hotel project management, real estate, energy, and even apparel.

M Corporation USA Ltd. has over the years established a reputation for excellence with hotel groups such as Starwood, Carlson, Marriot, IHG, the Hilton Group and many others. Our company has overseen the management, designing, construction and furnishing of many hotel, residential and commercial buildings all over the globe from the USA to China and still continues to do so.

M Corporation USA Ltd. is known to take on projects of any size and scope, whether it is advisory services for a small condo or project management on an entire building, our company will never shy away from a project regardless of its size. We also specialize in real estate and have demonstrated our expertise in designing and building dream homes and offices for our clients. M Corporation listens and translates clients' ideas and desires into a vision that can be transformed to reality.

Not only do we work with commercial buildings and hotels we are also involved with our strategic energy company partners and plan are ever expanding in Bangladesh and the rest of the region.

With the non-profit wing of M Corporation USA Ltd. we are eager to play an important role and set an example in the community, by helping and feeding the hunger in anyway we can.
