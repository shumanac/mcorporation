Mission Statements







  with more than 30 years of experience, is a world leading power company based in Spain that specializes in designing, creating and building power plants globally.

With many years of experience in building power plants and numerous projects across the world, TSK is a global leader when it comes to creating energy in places where it is needed most.

TSK has been building a variety of power plants which show the commitment and diversity it poses as a company. TSK is not solely specialized in building eclectic power plants as they are also capable of building and creating various others such as: hydro, solar and wind power plants. Furthermore TSK also specializes in power plant maintenance and local capacity building to enable sustainability of projects upon completion.

M Corporation USA Ltd. And TSK have merged together as strategic partners to work together in Bangladesh and the greater region of Asia. M Corporation has been the logistic partner of TSK on the finished Ashugan, Bangladesh power plant project and will be continuing to established itself together in Bangladesh and the region, with many more projects to come.

With projects and experience in virtually every imaginable field of electrical construction — design/build services, industrial and commercial developments as well as government, institutional, communications and utilities projects, our joint mission is to supply energy, where energy is scarce

Our success comes largely from the effort and dedication of our highly qualified and committed management team.

The philosophy we hold in high regard and have successfully incorporated is that our success is the dedication and motivation of our employees and their ability to remain highly competitive while staying profitable in a field where competition is abundant and fierce.
